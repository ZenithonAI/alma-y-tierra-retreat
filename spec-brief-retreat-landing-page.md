# Spec Brief: Yoga Retreat Landing Page

## Context
This is a spec project — not a real client. You're building this to:
1. Add a polished landing page to your Upwork portfolio
2. Develop your repeatable delivery process
3. Have a live demo to show potential clients

Based on a real Upwork listing for a therapeutic yoga retreat in Oaxaca, Mexico.

---

## The Brief (as if from a client)

**Project:** Landing page for a week-long therapeutic yoga and cultural retreat in Oaxaca, Mexico (June 21-27, 2026).

**Goal:** Get visitors to enquire about or book the retreat.

**Target audience:** Women aged 30-55 who are stressed professionals looking for a transformative wellness experience. They have disposable income and value authenticity over luxury.

---

## Page Layout (Sections)

### 1. Hero Section
- Large full-screen image (use a placeholder or free stock image of a colonial courtyard, colourful Oaxaca wall, or quiet yoga moment)
- Text overlay: "Oaxaca Therapeutic Yoga & Cultural Retreat"
- Date: June 21-27, 2026
- Small line: "A week of healing, movement, and discovery in the heart of Mexico"
- CTA button: "Reserve Your Spot"

### 2. Introduction / About the Retreat
- 2-3 short paragraphs describing the experience
- Focus on transformation, not just relaxation
- Mention: daily yoga, cultural excursions, local cuisine, small group (max 12 people)

### 3. What's Included
- Accommodation (boutique guesthouse)
- Daily morning and evening yoga sessions
- Guided cultural tours (markets, artisan workshops, Zapotec ruins)
- All meals (locally sourced, traditional Oaxacan cuisine)
- Airport transfers
- One-on-one wellness consultation

### 4. The Schedule (Day-by-Day Overview)
- Day 1: Arrival & Welcome Ceremony
- Day 2: Morning yoga + Monte Alban ruins excursion
- Day 3: Sunrise meditation + Oaxacan cooking class
- Day 4: Deep stretch session + Free afternoon in the city
- Day 5: Forest bathing + Evening sound healing
- Day 6: Community yoga + Farewell dinner
- Day 7: Departure

### 5. Meet Your Guide
- Name, photo placeholder, short bio
- Credentials (RYT-500, 10+ years teaching, trained in Bali and India)
- Personal philosophy quote

### 6. Testimonials
- 3 testimonials from past attendees (create realistic fictional ones)
- Include first name, location, and a short quote

### 7. Pricing
- Early Bird: $2,400 (before April 30)
- Standard: $2,800
- What's NOT included: flights, travel insurance, personal shopping
- Deposit: $500 to reserve

### 8. FAQ Section
- 4-6 common questions (skill level needed, solo travellers welcome, dietary requirements, cancellation policy)

### 9. Final CTA / Contact
- "Ready to Transform Your Practice?"
- Simple contact form: Name, Email, Message
- Or: "Email us at hello@oaxacaretreat.com"

---

## Design Direction

**Vibe:** Warm, earthy, premium but not corporate. Think boutique travel magazine, not generic wellness site.

**Colour palette:**
- Warm terracotta / burnt orange
- Deep forest green
- Cream / warm white
- Charcoal for text

**Typography:**
- Display/heading: Something distinctive and elegant (serif or display serif)
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif

**Imagery style:** Warm tones, golden hour light, authentic Mexican textures (textiles, pottery, architecture), candid yoga moments (not overly posed studio shots).

**Layout:** Single page, smooth scroll. Generous white space. Full-width image sections alternating with text sections.

---

## Technical Requirements

- **Stack:** Next.js + Tailwind CSS (your standard stack)
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first, looks great on all devices
- **Deployed:** Vercel
- **Performance:** Fast load, optimised images
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll animations (fade-in sections)
- **Form:** Simple contact form (can use Formspree or similar for the demo)

## Your Delivery Process (Build This Into a Template)

Use this project to establish your standard workflow:

### Step 1: Discovery (Day 1)
- Read the brief
- Ask clarifying questions (for spec: answer them yourself)
- Collect any assets (images, copy, brand guidelines)

### Step 2: Design Direction (Day 1-2)
- Choose colour palette, typography, layout approach
- Create a quick mood reference (even just 3-4 screenshots of sites you're drawing inspiration from)

### Step 3: Build (Day 2-4)
- Set up the project (Next.js + Tailwind)
- Build mobile-first
- Section by section, top to bottom
- Use placeholder images initially

### Step 4: Polish (Day 4-5)
- Add animations and micro-interactions
- Optimise for mobile
- Test all breakpoints
- Replace placeholder content with final copy
- Deploy to Vercel

### Step 5: Delivery
- Share live link

- Ask for feedback / revisions
